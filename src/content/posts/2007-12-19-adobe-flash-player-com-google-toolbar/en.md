In 2007, while setting up computers for a LAN gaming center, I noticed that the Adobe Flash Player installer also offered an optional installation of Google Toolbar.

> *The original installer screenshots were no longer available during the migration.*

The offer stood out because it showed the reach of Flash Player at the time. The Google Toolbar features I used most included:

- autocomplete in search;
- bookmarks tied to a Google account;
- Orkut search.

The installer and the products mentioned here have since been discontinued. This text is preserved as a record of the web in 2007.
