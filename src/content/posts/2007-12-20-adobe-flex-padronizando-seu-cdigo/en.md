Code standards matter in both team development and individual projects. Consistent conventions provide several benefits:

- better readability;
- more predictable maintenance;
- easier code sharing;
- less accidental variation between modules and authors.

In 2007, Fábio Terracini from the [FlexDev](http://groups.google.com.br/group/flexdev) community published a convention guide for MXML and ActionScript at DClick. The material was also discussed on the international FlexCoders list.
