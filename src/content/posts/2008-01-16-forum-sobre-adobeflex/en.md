In January 2008, we announced a forum for the Brazilian Adobe Flex community. Built with phpBB 3, it aimed to connect:

- beginners looking for help;
- professionals interested in sharing knowledge;
- people who wanted to promote opportunities and projects.

The original address, `forum.flexbrasil.com.br`, is no longer active. This post remains as a record of the community at that time.
